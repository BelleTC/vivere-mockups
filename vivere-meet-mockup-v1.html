Planning Your Event

How to Book an Event at Vivere Hotel

Three straightforward steps — and a dedicated event associate with you through every one.

01

Get in Touch

Reach our events team by phone, email, or through our online inquiry form. Share your event date, expected number of guests, and the type of occasion — and we'll confirm availability right away.

02

Plan Together

Your dedicated event associate will walk you through venue options, layout configurations, catering menus, and event packages — all tailored to your goals, your headcount, and your budget.

03

Leave It to Us

On event day, our banquet and events team handles every detail — from setup and service to teardown. You focus on your meeting or your milestone. We'll make sure it runs perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Events at Vivere Hotel

Quick answers about function rooms, capacity, bookings, and what Vivere Hotel offers for both corporate meetings and celebrations in Alabang.

Vivere Hotel has 12 function rooms and event spaces spread across two floors. The 2nd floor houses the Main Ballroom, Vivere Ballroom, Angkiukok, Mango, Zosima, Brasserie, Patio, and Exclusive rooms. The 31st floor offers sky-level venues including Main Dining, Picasso, Patio, and Exclusive — with panoramic views of the Metro South skyline.

Vivere Hotel's event venues can accommodate up to 200 guests for banquet-style functions. Smaller venues are also available for intimate meetings, private dinners, and gatherings of fewer than 20 persons. Your event associate can recommend the right venue based on your headcount and setup requirements.

All venues include A/V setup, high-speed Wi-Fi, and professional in-house catering.

Vivere Hotel is located at 5102 Bridgeway Avenue, Filinvest City, Alabang, Muntinlupa City 1781. It is easily accessible from SLEX, Alabang-Zapote Road, and the Filinvest commercial district — making it a convenient event venue for guests from Makati, BGC, Las Piñas, Parañaque, and Cavite.

Good to Know

Before You Arrive

Check-in begins at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 noon. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged and are subject to room availability. Please contact our reservations team in advance to request either — direct booking guests receive room preference priority, which helps us accommodate these requests when possible.

Breakfast inclusion depends on the rate booked. Guests booking directly through viverehotel.com.ph or our reservations team receive a complimentary breakfast buffet at The Nest for the number of registered adults. If you booked via a third-party platform, please refer to your booking confirmation. High-speed Wi-Fi is complimentary for all in-house guests regardless of rate.

Yes. Room Preference Priority is a direct booking exclusive — you may indicate your preferred floor, bed configuration, or view in the special requests field when booking, or contact reservations directly at reservations@viverehotel.com.ph. All requests are honored based on availability at the time of check-in.

Yes. The rooftop pool is exclusively for registered in-house guests and is open daily from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Pool towels are provided. Children are welcome and must be accompanied by an adult at all times. Day use access is not available.

Flexible rate bookings made directly may be cancelled without charge up to 72 hours before your scheduled arrival date. Cancellations within 72 hours may incur a one-night penalty. Promotional and non-refundable rates follow separate terms stated at the time of booking. For any changes, contact our reservations team at reservations@viverehotel.com.ph or call +63 977 847-2799.

Steal away to The Nest Dining in the Sky, Vivere Hotel's iconic rooftop bar and restaurant. Hovering 31 storeys above the Metro South, The Nest commands a breathtaking panorama of the skyline — a magical setting for making memories that last.

Our menu is a rich selection of international cuisine, continuously innovated by our R&D team with a focus on comfort and discovery. Pair your meal with hand-crafted cocktails — from the sangria that's become a guest favourite, to the signatures our bar team builds fresh each season.

Whether you are visiting solo, dining with family, or hosting a celebration, The Nest offers cozy corners and sweeping views in equal measure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Dining at Vivere Hotel

Answers about The Nest Dining in the Sky, hours, reservations, events, and all dining options at Vivere Hotel Alabang.

The Nest Dining in the Sky is on the 31st floor of Vivere Hotel, located at 5102 Bridgeway Avenue, Filinvest City, Alabang, Muntinlupa City. It is one of the highest rooftop restaurants in the Metro South.

The Nest Dining in the Sky is open daily from 4:00 PM to 11:00 PM. No dress code required, though smart casual is appreciated for the setting.

Reservations are not required but are highly encouraged to guarantee your seating, especially on weekends and during peak evenings. You can reserve online at tinyurl.com/TheNestReservation or contact us at thenest@viverehotel.com.ph or +63 917 804-3257.

The Nest serves international cuisine with Filipino and Asian Fusion influences, continuously innovated by our R&D team. The menu focuses on comfort food done well — paired with hand-crafted cocktails, house sangria, and signature drinks. View the full menu here.

Yes. The Nest can accommodate small groups to large occasions of up to 150 persons. Private dining is available for corporate dinners, romantic occasions, and small celebrations. For parties of 20 persons and above, please contact our team directly at thenest@viverehotel.com.ph or +63 917 804-3257.

Yes. The Nest regularly features live music performances that complement the panoramic skyline views. Check with our team at thenest@viverehotel.com.ph or follow @viverehotel for the latest schedules.

Vivere Hotel has four dining destinations: The Nest Dining in the Sky (rooftop restaurant and bar, 31F, 4PM–11PM daily), Shogun Japanese Restaurant (open daily 11AM–11PM), Lama Pool Place (rooftop pool bar, light bites and cocktails, in-house guests only), and The Nest Nook (private dining area within The Nest, by reservation).

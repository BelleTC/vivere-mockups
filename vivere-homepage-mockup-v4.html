About Vivere Hotel

25 years of genuine Filipino hospitality.

"Purposeful stays, timeless moments." — Vivere Hotel, Alabang.

Vivere Hotel sits at the heart of Filinvest Corporate City in Alabang, Muntinlupa — a short drive from Ninoy Aquino International Airport and a few minutes' walk from Festival Mall and Alabang Town Center. It's the kind of hotel that business travelers return to, families choose for milestone moments, and Manila locals pick for a proper staycation without the pretense.

Expect thoughtfully appointed rooms, a rooftop restaurant with a view, a calm pool deck, and a team that knows your name. Comfort that's felt, not performed.

Frequently Asked

Good to know before you arrive.

Where is Vivere Hotel located?+
Vivere Hotel is at 5102 Bridgeway Ave. cor. Asean Drive, Filinvest Corporate City, Alabang, Muntinlupa City. It is approximately 15 minutes from Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) by car, a 1-minute walk from Festival Mall, and around 17 minutes from Alabang Town Center. The hotel sits at the heart of Filinvest City — Alabang's premier business and lifestyle district in the Metro South.
What are the check-in and check-out times?+
Standard check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 noon. The front desk is open 24 hours. Early check-in (from 12:00 noon) and late check-out (until 2:00 PM) may be arranged upon request, subject to room availability on the day. Guests arriving early are welcome to leave their luggage with the concierge at no charge.
Does Vivere Hotel have a swimming pool?+
Yes. The infinity pool is located on the 31st floor alongside the fitness center, and is exclusively available to all registered hotel guests. The pool deck offers an elevated view of the Filinvest City skyline — a popular spot for both morning laps and sunset relaxation.
How do I get the best room rate?+
The lowest guaranteed rates are available when you book directly through viverehotel.com.ph or via our SwiftBook engine. Direct bookings also include exclusive perks: breakfast buffet inclusion, 10% off dinner reservations at The Nest, 25% off after-hours drink and dine, room preference priority, and a discount at other Vivere Hospitality Group properties. For personal assistance, call +63 977 847-2799 or email reservations@viverehotel.com.ph.
What dining options are available at the hotel?+
Vivere Hotel has four dining destinations. The Nest Dining in the Sky is the rooftop restaurant and bar on the 31st floor — serving breakfast buffet daily from 5:30 to 10:00 AM, and dinner from 4:00 PM onwards. The bar stays open until 1:00 AM on Thursdays through Saturdays. Shogun Japanese Restaurant is open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Lama Pool Place offers casual poolside dining on the 31st floor. The Nest Nook is a daytime cafe and co-working lounge for guests seeking a quieter setting.
